What are the benefits of the CPZ?


Reduces congestion

Limits long-stay and commuter parking, freeing up space for local drivers and reducing traffic searching for parking. It also frees up the roads for service and emergency vehicles which require access.

Prioritises local access

Ensures parking spaces are more likely to be available for residents, businesses, and short-term visitors.

Encourages sustainable transport

Makes walking, cycling, and public transport more attractive by discouraging unnecessary car use. Local bus services will improve as more people use them.

Improves road safety

Reduces instances of pavement and junction parking, improving visibility and safety for all road users. The streets close to the beach in particular will be safer for families and children to walk around.

Supports cleaner air

Fewer vehicles circulating and idling contributes to better air quality and a healthier street environment. Reduced traffic noise has been proven to have a positive impact on mental health and wellbeing.
