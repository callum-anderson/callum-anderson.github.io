What is the Portobello CPZ?


The Portobello CPZ - or Controlled Parking Zone - is a proposal put forward by the City of Edinburgh Council to address parking concerns in the Portobello area. These concerns were raised following a public survey, carried out in 2021.

The proposal and consultation can be viewed at:

https://www.edinburgh.gov.uk/parking/strategic-parking-review-1/3

https://consultation.appyway.com/Edinburgh-City


The proposal has been shared with residents and businesses as part of the consultation. All responses will be forwarded to the councilâ€™s Traffic Regulation Order Sub Committee, which is expected to complete by Autumn 2025. The consultation is open until 23rd May 2025.
