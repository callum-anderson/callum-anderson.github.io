Welcome...

Hello! My name is Callum Anderson, and I'm a member of Cohort E24 at Codeclan. I've been asked to create this webpage to let everyone know a bit more about me!

I've worked in the NHS for the past decade, within various administrative and IT roles. Prior to that, I worked in retail for a number of years, after dropping out of Uni. That's certainly one of my regrets - but you can only influence the future, not the past!

More recently, I've been involved in project work, which has piqued my interest in software development. Having seen the impact of quality* software in a busy, working environment, I decided that I want to learn how to deliver solutions - rather than just help to implement them.

  * I've also seen the utter chaos caused by "less than useful" software!

I also enjoy "tinkering" with tech at home, and have a few ideas for some cool projects after I learn to code.
