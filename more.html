About Me

I thought I'd list a few facts about myself here, which I hope you'll find vaguely interesting!
  • I lived in England until I was 7, when my parents divorced and my Dad brought us (myself & my two brothers) up to live in Scotland
  • My mother's side of the family live in Northamptonshire, so I regularly make the 660 mile round trip down the M1/M6 to visit
  • I live with my brother (for now!!) in Tranent - about 10 miles east of Edinburgh
  • I moved to Milton Keynes in 2017, but returned to Scotland at the beginning of the year. I may move back one day...
  • My biggest passion is music. I used to DJ regularly, but I'm semi-retired at the moment! I spend far, far too long organising my music collection...
  • My other interests include wildlife and nature. I'm hoping to eventually retire to the countryside, in the middle of nowhere!
  • I am 95% vegan, the 5% means I cheat on occasion...
