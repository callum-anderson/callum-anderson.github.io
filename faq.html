A CPZ is an area where parking is regulated during certain hours to reduce congestion, discourage commuter parking, and prioritize space for residents and local businesses.

Only vehicles with a valid permit or those using a pay-and-display system can park during controlled hours. Outside of those hours, parking is usually unrestricted.
